Teen SOYO

A Place to Belong

Teen SOYO is where our youth build real friendships and find a strong sense of belonging in the Church. In a world that often pulls young people in every direction, SOYO offers a Christ-centered community where teens can be themselves, feel supported, and grow together. It’s not just a social group—it’s a place where faith becomes personal.

Growing as Leaders in the Church

SOYO also encourages teens to participate in parish life and grow into leadership through service, worship, and fellowship. Whether it’s helping with events, joining discussions, serving the community, or learning more about Orthodox life, SOYO helps young people gain confidence in their faith and understand that the Church is truly their home—now and for the future.
