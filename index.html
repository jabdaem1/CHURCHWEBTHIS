How You Can Contribute

St. George’s mission is to offer our community a place in which they can come together with other Orthodox Christians to receive moral, ethical, and spiritual guidance based on our Orthodox Christian faith, providing a foundation for leading a Christ-centered life, and giving opportunities to spread love, joy, and Hope to our neighbors through works of mercy and outreach. This is made possible by donations from generous people such as yourself. Anything you are able to offer will benefit the ministries here at St. George.
